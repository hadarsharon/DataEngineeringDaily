Background

Data Engineering Daily, operating since 2016, is a primarily data-oriented technology news website, founded with the goal of being the leading source for Data-related news, as well as providing guides, tutorials and other supplementary orientation material for aspiring & experienced Data Engineers alike.
Since the site's inception, we have been active in promoting and covering numerous Data Engineering and/or Open Source related conferences - namely:  The IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering (ICDE),  Open Source Summit (formerly known as LinuxCon), and  DevConf, among others.
Perhaps more importantly - site readers are also encouraged to apply their knowledge and contribute to Open Source Data-related projects! 
Some well-known examples of aforementioned projects include:  Apache Airflow,  Great Expectations and  the Julia Programming Language).
(One could get acquainted, at his/her discretion, with such technologies & tools by exploring our Guides & Tutorials section
